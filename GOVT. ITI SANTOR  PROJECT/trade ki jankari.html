Hamare Trades ki Jankari

1. Electrician (इलेक्ट्रीशियन)

Electrician trade mein students ko bijli se jude upkaran (appliances), wiring, motor winding aur power distribution ke baare mein sikhaya jaata hai. Yeh ek evergreen trade hai.

2. Fitter (फिटर)

Fitter trade mein machines ke parts ko assemble karna, unki repairing aur maintenance karna sikhaya jaata hai. Industrial production mein fitters ki bahut maang hoti hai.

3. COPA (Computer Operator & Programming Assistant)

Is trade mein computer software, hardware, internet, data entry aur basic programming sikhayi jaati hai. IT sector mein career banane ke liye yeh best hai.

4. Dress Making (ड्रेस मेकिंग)

Fashion aur textile industry mein interest rakhne waalon ke liye yeh trade hai. Isme design, stitching aur kapdon ki cutting ki professional training di jaati hai.

5. Welder (वेल्डर)

Welder trade mein metal parts ko jodne (welding) ki alag-alag techniques sikhayi jaati hain. Construction aur manufacturing industries mein iska kaam bahut hai.

6. Plumber (प्लंबर)

Plumber trade mein pipes, drainage systems aur water supply ki fitting aur repair sikhayi jaati hai. Domestic aur industrial dono jagah iski zarurat padti hai.
