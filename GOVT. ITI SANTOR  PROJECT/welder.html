Welder (Gas & Electric)

Future of Metal Joining Technology

Trade Overview (ट्रेड की जानकारी)

Welder trade ITI ka ek mahatvapurn technical course hai. Isme students ko vibhinn prakar ki welding techniques jaise Gas, Arc, TIG, aur MIG welding sikhayi jati hai jo ki aaj ki industrial duniya ki backbone hai.

📅 Course Duration

Duration: 1 Year (2 Semesters)

Eligibility: 8th / 10th Pass

Certificate: NCVT / SCVT

🛠️ What You Will Learn

  • Arc & Gas Welding Techniques
  • Gas Cutting & Brazing
  • TIG & MIG Welding
  • Safety Precisions & PPE
  • Blue-print Reading

🚀 Career Scope

  • Railways & Metro Projects
  • Automobile Industry
  • Oil & Gas Pipelines
  • Shipbuilding & Aerospace
  • Self-Employment (Own Workshop)
