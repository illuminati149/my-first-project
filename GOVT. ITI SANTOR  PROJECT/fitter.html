Fitter Trade Kya Hai? (About Fitter)

Fitter trade ITI ki sabse purani aur lokpriya trades mein se ek hai. Isme students ko machine ke parts ko assemble karna, repair karna aur maintain karna sikhaya jata hai. Ek fitter metal parts ko finishing dekar unhe machine mein fit karne ka expert hota hai.

Kya Kya Seekhenge? (Skills Covered)

  • Marking, Filing aur Fitting
  • Lathe Machine aur Drilling
  • Welding aur Gas Cutting
  • Measurement Tools (Vernier, Micrometer)
  • Hydraulics aur Pneumatics basics

Career Opportunities

  • Indian Railways (Technician)
  • BHEL, ONGC, SAIL, GAIL
  • Automobile Industry (Maruti, Tata)
  • Self-Employment (Own Workshop)
  • Maintenance Fitter in Factories
