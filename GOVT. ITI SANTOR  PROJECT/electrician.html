Electrician Trade

Powering the World, One Connection at a Time

Trade Overview (ट्रेड का परिचय)

Electrician ITI ek 2-saal ka vocational course hai jo students ko electrical systems, wiring, aur machinery ke baare mein expert banata hai. Is course mein theory se zyada practical knowledge par focus kiya jata hai.

Course Details

Duration (अवधि) 2 Years (4 Semesters)
Eligibility (योग्यता) 10th Pass (Science & Maths preferred)
Certification NCVT / SCVT

Aap Kya Seekhenge? (Syllabus Highlights)

House Wiring

Gharon aur building mein safe wiring karna seekhein.

Motor Winding

DC/AC Motors aur Generators ki repair aur winding.

Solar Panel

Renewable energy aur solar system installation.

Industrial Controls

Factories mein use hone wali badi machines ki control wiring.

Career Opportunities (करियर के अवसर)
